Timeline

Calendar



Each class takes 3 weeks to complete. You can enroll in a maximum of 2 classes at any time. there are 21 instructional weeks in semester 1 (January to May). Thus one may complete 7 classes per semester at regular pace, and 14 classes at an advanced pace. This allows students who fail a class to repeat it immediately.

Saturdays are always open for students to come and get help. It is an informal day of high teacher to student ratios, with games to play, and movies to watch.

Sunday is a day of rest, and school is closed for academic purposes.

Semester 2 begins in the last week of July, and also runs 21 weeks.
